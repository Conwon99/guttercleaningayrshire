Frequently Asked Questions

How often should gutters be cleaned in Ayrshire?

Most Ayrshire homes benefit from a gutter clean once or twice a year — typically in late autumn after the leaves have fallen, and again in spring after winter storms. Properties with overhanging trees or a lot of nearby pines may need more regular visits.

Can blocked gutters cause damp or leaks?

Yes. When gutters block, rainwater spills down the wall instead of into the downpipe. Over time this soaks into the brickwork or render and causes damp patches inside. Clearing gutters regularly is one of the cheapest ways to prevent damp and water damage.

Do you clean downpipes as well?

Yes. A gutter is only really clean when the water has somewhere to go. We check, flush and unblock downpipes as part of every gutter clean.

Can you clean gutters on commercial properties?

Yes. We work with landlords, facilities managers and business owners across Ayrshire on shops, offices, units and schools — both one-off and planned regular visits.
